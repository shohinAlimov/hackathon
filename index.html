01 — Problem & Solution

Two problems.
One elegant solution.

⚠️

The Problems

City streets are polluted — plastic waste, litter, and poor recycling habits damage public health

Urban transport is heavily car-dependent — adding to emissions and traffic congestion

People want to take eco-action but lack tangible, immediate incentive to do so

Bike-sharing systems exist but adoption remains low without compelling on-ramps

🚲

Our Solution

Earn EcoPoints by completing verified eco-actions — recycling, cleaning up, reducing plastic

EcoPoints are real currency — spend them to unlock and pay for bicycle rides across the city

Photo proof + AI verification ensures every action is genuine, not gamed

Team competitions and leaderboards drive communities to collectively improve their neighborhoods
