01 — Problem & Solution

The world's biggest challenge
needs everyday heroes

⚠️

The Problem

People want to help the environment but lack motivation and direction

Environmental campaigns feel abstract — no immediate feedback or reward

Individual actions feel insignificant without visible community impact

No gamified platform connects real-world eco actions to visible progress

🌱

Our Solution

Earn EcoPoints for verified real-life actions (recycling, planting, saving water)

Points unlock buildings in a shared virtual city — making impact visible

Team competition drives collective motivation and social accountability

Leaderboards, streaks, and achievements keep engagement high long-term
