04 — Process

How partnership works

From first contact to measurable impact in under 2 weeks.

1

Apply & Choose a Package

Fill out the partner form below. We'll schedule a 30-minute call to understand your goals and match you with the right tier.

2

Onboarding & Branding Setup

We set up your branded team space, add your logo to the leaderboard, and configure any custom eco-action categories you need.

3

Launch & Activate Your Team

Share a partner invite link with your employees or community. They join your team, start completing verified eco-actions, and earn EcoPoints for real bike rides.

4

Track Impact in Real Time

Your dashboard shows live participation, eco-action counts, rides unlocked, and CO₂ offset estimates — all exportable for ESG reporting.

5

Monthly Impact Report

Receive a branded PDF impact report every month — shareable with stakeholders, investors, or regulatory bodies as proof of ESG commitment.
